About Us

Who are we?

We are a student-run peer-tutoring organization, sponsored by Mr. Crouch, dedicated to providing resources to the ESL students at PESH through biweekly tutoring sessions. We also offer Volunteer hours for tutors.

Where we are

Currently, we are located at Plano East Senior High School. But due to our apparent success, we have begun this tutoring program at other schools like Armstrong Middle School.

Why we are important

Peer tutoring provides a chance to help a student who may be struggling academically. Moreover, it is an opportunity to step outside of one's comfort zone, meet new people, and make new experiences.
